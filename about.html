About Us

Pizza Days has been serving up fresh, hot pizza since 2005, when the first location opened in Burlington, MA. Our Medford shop sits right across the street from Tufts University on Boston Avenue, making us the go-to spot for students, families, and late-night cravers alike.

We're open until 3 AM every night because we believe great pizza shouldn't have a curfew. Whether it's a loaded Meat Lover's pie, a crispy calzone, or a classic cheese slice, everything is made fresh and delivered fast.

Beyond our storefront, Pizza Days also partners with local schools through our School Lunch Program, delivering meals that meet national nutritional guidelines. From midnight runs to midday lunches, we're proud to feed our community.
