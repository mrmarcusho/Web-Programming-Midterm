About Pizza Days

Our story, our passion, our pizza

Serving Great Pizza Since 2005

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved local pizza chain with four locations and thousands of happy customers.

Our Story

Pizza Days was founded by the Moretti family in 2005 with a simple vision: make the best pizza in town using real, fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

What began as a single storefront on Main Street quickly became a neighborhood favorite. Word spread about our hand-tossed dough, house-made sauce, and generous toppings. Within five years, we opened our second location, and by 2015, Pizza Days had grown to four thriving stores across Springfield.

Today, we still make every pizza the same way — by hand, with care, and with the freshest ingredients we can find. Some things never change, and that is exactly how our customers like it.

Our Values

Quality First

We never cut corners on ingredients. From our dough to our toppings, everything is made fresh and sourced from trusted suppliers.

Community

We are proud to be a part of Springfield. We sponsor local sports teams, donate to food drives, and support neighborhood events year-round.

Family

Pizza Days is a family business, and we treat every customer like family. From our dining room to your living room, we want you to feel at home.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly packaging, minimize food waste, and partner with local farms to reduce our environmental footprint.

Meet the Team

Behind every great pizza is a dedicated team. Our skilled pizza makers, friendly counter staff, and reliable delivery drivers work together to bring you the best experience possible. We currently employ over 80 team members across our four locations — and we are always looking for passionate people to join the Pizza Days family.

Interested in working with us? Get in touch — we would love to hear from you.
